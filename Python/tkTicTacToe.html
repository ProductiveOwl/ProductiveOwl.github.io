Tic Tac Toe + GUI

In this program, the user and another player plays the classic game of Tic Tac Toe. The players click on the grid to mark their spot. The first person to get three in a row/column/diagonal wins! To reset the game, rerun the program. Make sure to look at the OUTPUT tab. May not work on all devices.
